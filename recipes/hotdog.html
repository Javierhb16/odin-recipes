Hotdog

Description

Hotdogs are eaten all around the world, due to its nice taste and quick preparation

Ingredients

  • 1 Sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Hotdog bun

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage completely
  2. Cut the hotdog bun in half, without dividing it at all
  3. Put the sausage in the middle of the bun
  4. Add ketchup and mustard (or whatever is desired)
  5. Enjoy
