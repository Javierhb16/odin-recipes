Pasta

Description

Pasta is an Italian dish which is quick and low resource to make.

Ingredients

  • Any type of pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a boiler with water
  2. Put the pasta on the boiler
  3. Stir the pasta until it is soft
  4. Put the pasta in a plate
  5. Add tomato sauce to the pasta and spread it
  6. Add cheese on top of it if you want
  7. Enjoy
