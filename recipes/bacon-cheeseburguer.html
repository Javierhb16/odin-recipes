Bacon Cheeseburguer

Description

A bacon cheeseburguer is a simple, tasty recipe that anyone can do at their homes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Burguer patty
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • Burguer bun

Steps

  1. Cook the burguer patty on one side
  2. Cook the three slices of bacon on both sides
  3. After flipping the patty, put both slices of cheese on top of it
  4. Cut the burguer bun in half
  5. Put the patty with the cheese on the bun
  6. Put the slices of bacon on top of the patty and the cheese
  7. Close the bun with the other half
  8. Enjoy
